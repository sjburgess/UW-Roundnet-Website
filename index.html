Welcome to the Badger Roundnet Club! Whether you're a seasoned player or just curious about the fast-paced, exciting game of Spikeball, you've come to the right place. Our club is dedicated to fostering a fun and inclusive community where players of all skill levels can improve their game, meet new people, and enjoy some friendly competition. We host regular practices, tournaments, and social events throughout the year, so come join us and get ready to spike your way to new friendships and unforgettable memories.
